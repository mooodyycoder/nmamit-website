Overview

NMAMIT offers a wide range of resources for its students, including a comprehensive Digital Library with extensive e-resources from major publishers, a fully automated KOHA-based Library, advanced research laboratories, and a dedicated placement department with support for both national and international opportunities. Students can also access shared materials through online platforms and utilize the college's student mentoring system for academic and personal support.

Library Resources

Digital Library & E-Resources:

Access to over 40,000+ e-resources from publishers like IEEE, Springer, and ProQuest, available through the VTU Consortium.

Automated Library System:

The library uses the KOHA open-source software and a barcode system for efficient management of physical books.

Collection:

A vast collection of 87,832 volumes of text and reference books.

Periodicals:

Subscription to 125 national and international periodicals for core engineering disciplines.
